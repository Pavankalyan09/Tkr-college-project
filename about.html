TKR College Of Engineering & Technology

TKR College of Engineering and Technology - a modern temple of learning, an off shoot of the TKR Educational Society was established in the year 2002 in a sprawling, lush green 20 acre campus at Meerpet, Hyderabad.

about us

TKR College of Engineering and Technology is located in Hyderabad, Telangana and was established in 2002. It is a premier institute in higher education and offers UG, PG and After 10th in Full Time mode in the form of Degree and Diploma programmes. The most popular programmes offered at TKR College of Engineering and Technology are B.E. / B.Tech, MBA/PGDM, M.E./M.Tech and After 10th Diploma programmes.

The institute offers AICTE approved programs to students in Full Time mode to pursue their careers in higher studies. Further, the institute offers a wide range of programmes in the streams of Engineering and Business & Management Studies

TKR Educational Society was established in 2001, it is reputedly academic oriented.

Sri. Teegala Krishna Reddy, the former Mayor of Hyderabad, Ex-MLA Maheshwaram and leader of masses is the Chairman of the society. A native of Meerpet Village with challenging experiences in the process of emerged.
